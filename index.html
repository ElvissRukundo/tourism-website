ABOUT US

Welcome to JPE Tours and Travel, where we invite you to embark on a journey to explore the breathtaking landscapes and rich cultural tapestry of Uganda. Nestled in the heart of East Africa, our country is renowned for its stunning national parks, diverse wildlife, and vibrant communities. Our mission is to provide personalized, adventurous tours that not only create unforgettable memories but also foster a deep connection between travelers and the authentic beauty of our homeland. With a team of experienced guides who are passionate about sharing their knowledge and love for Uganda, we strive to deliver exceptional experiences tailored to your interests. Whether you’re seeking thrilling wildlife encounters, cultural immersions, or serene escapes into nature, JPE Tours and Travel is dedicated to curating the perfect itinerary for you. Join us as we unveil the hidden gems of Uganda, ensuring every moment is filled with joy, discovery, and adventure.

How it Began

Our journey started with a deep passion for exploring Uganda’s hidden gems, transforming that love into a mission to share these wonders with travelers.

Our Services

Guided Tours

Discover Uganda with our expert guides. Tailored tours ensure an immersive experience in our stunning landscapes and vibrant cultures.

Photography Tours

Capture the breathtaking beauty of Uganda with professional guidance. Perfect for all skill levels, enhance your photography skills while exploring.

Adventure Activities

Experience thrilling activities like white-water rafting and zip-lining. Safe and fun adventures for nature lovers and adrenaline seekers.

Boat Safaris

Glide through Uganda’s waterways on unforgettable boat safaris. Enjoy wildlife viewing while our guides share insights into the local ecosystem.

Cultural Experiences

Engage with local communities and learn about their traditions. Participate in cultural ceremonies for a deeper understanding of Ugandan life.

Culinary Tours

Savor Ugandan cuisine with our culinary tours. Explore local markets, learn cooking methods, and indulge in the country’s diverse flavors.

Car Hire Services

Rent a car for your adventures in Uganda. Choose from a variety of vehicles that suit your travel needs and explore at your own pace.

Gorilla Trekking Safaris

Embark on a journey to see the majestic mountain gorillas in their natural habitat. A once-in-a-lifetime experience for wildlife lovers.
